Why assassins noodles?

Back in my assasssin days, right after i destroyed JFK, i lived in Italy. Being an assassin, all they will really feed you is these oily, bloody noodles. Haha, they are not made in blood, but a thick gravy (also known as marinara)

Assassin's noodles are an easy weekday meal, that people of all ages will love... especially if you are an assassin. Enjoy!
